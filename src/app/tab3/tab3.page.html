Credits Dedicated the well-paid consultants working for the Swiss government. Purpose: my Personal Passive Protest

The Observation

I was always puzzled how we at the Swiss government fail to keep performing IT staff. It angers me that not just innovators and cracks, but also the average Joe quits his employment and to return as high-waged1 consultants at another federal org unit. It's a reoccuring pattern and it stinks2.

A Utopian Proposal

I suggest, we fail to compensate in an adequate way. Adequate is subjective? not necesarrily. A environment where there is no common understanding why one salary is higher than an other, baits misunderstanding, makes workers feel treated biased. Subsequently, if we don't bring arguments and salary rule sets to the surface we can NOT expect a common understanding. If employees with lower salary cannot understand why their salary is composed to a lower salary than the one from a comparable collegue, discontent will arise and resistance will sprout. Despite transparency ask for a hight effort, it's worth it.
It also offers to let go of employees that grumble and moan for neglectable loan difference of a few pennies. This group will NOT perform, not even after a salary rise or adjustment. It's a chance to let got of them, while there is a chance to keep the performers and maybe come to an agreement after understanding THEIR perspectives. And yes, I have seen wage-limits breach after calm discussions, where solid work over years proceeded the talk.

Don't feel offended

After all my protest is neither meant personal nor targets individuals. It merely trails my enduring hope of finding a contemporary way for the government and inspiring knowledge workers to persist as a team. All for Operational Excellence, so that tax payers more likely enjoys Paying her/his tax bill.
